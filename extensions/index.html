Some instructions

How to use trains and routes

Open the main menu and go to "routes". Then choose the route you need in the folders of your home folder.

How to drive Trains

First press "X" to start the motor. after that choose the direction of the train by pressing "F" to go forward or "V" to go backwards. To increase the spped prees "," as often as you need. To stop press "."
